Who I Am

My name is Lucas. I am student at the University of Wisconsin - Madison studying Computer Science, with a minor in Digital Communications. I want to work for a company that perpetuates creativity and authenticity. A company that looks at their employees as more than just worker bees, one which invests in them to develop their skills and become better professionals. One which trusts their employees to do quality work, and do it timely.

Understanding what I am looking for, the question arises: what do I bring the table? First off, attitude. I have been told that I am a positive force to work with, with an electric personality. I do not want to be a toxic environment, and I certainly do not want to contribute to it. Secondly, leadership. Achieving the rank of Eagle has taught me many things. One of these being the EDGE Method. EDGE stands for Educate, Demonstrate, Guide, and Enable. I apply this as often as possible as it allows for deep relationships, and further understanding of content. And finally, my work ethic. I hate letting people down, and I hate not meeting expectations. I am proud of my work, but I always seek to do better.

But work is not all that I am about. What do I do in my freetime? I love gaming. It is what originally got me into computers. Playing games with friends around the world is something that I love to do to pass time. Playing through stories, puzzles, and entertaining enviroments keeps me creative and continuously thinking. But while PC games are great, I am also an avid Dungeons and Dragons player. I love rolling the dice and slaying mythical beasts. I am both a Dungeon Master of my own group, as well as player. Outside of my apartment and away from my desktop, I love mountain biking and scuba diving.
